"Love to capture extraordinary moments to get beautiful memories"

My name is Arung, and I am a freelance photographer specializing in event documentation, portraits, and pre-wedding photography. To me, a captivating photograph is not merely a compilation of beautiful images; it's a mosaic of cherished moments frozen in time, transforming into unforgettable memories. In the rush of life, we often overlook these precious moments, and that's where I come in.

Witnessing a powerful photo, I realized the profound impact it can have. It goes beyond aesthetics; it encapsulates the essence of the beautiful instants we sometimes fail to immortalize. My mission is to capture your remarkable moments and transform them into timeless memories. This dedication is what fuels my passion for photography.

Whether it's documenting events, capturing individual portraits, or crafting the perfect pre-wedding shots, I am committed to creating images that tell a story, evoke emotions, and etch your memories in the canvas of time. Let's embark on this journey together, turning your wonderful moments into beautiful memories that last a lifetime."
